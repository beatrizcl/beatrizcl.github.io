About Me

Software Developer with solid experience in Robotic Process Automation (RPA), backend development, and systems integration. I have worked both as an in-house developer and as a freelance RPA consultant for companies in Brazil and abroad.

Publications

FUZZY: Development of a Shell for the Construction of Diffuse Systems Models

Riuni – UNISUL · Jun 23, 2020

This article presents the development of a Shell for the automatic generation of Fuzzy systems, which allows the definition of linguistic variables, relevance degrees, and rules used in the Fuzzification and De-fuzzification processes. It was tested by Computer Science students at Unisul and compared to a consolidated framework. Results showed its effectiveness in handling imprecise data and supporting decisions in fuzzy systems.
